# Compliance Overview

This project is designed to satisfy stringent 2026-era AI regulations and standards, including:

- **EU AI Act** – Articles 6, 12, 14, 16, and 72 for high-risk AI systems.
- **GDPR** – Especially the Right to Erasure (cryptographic shredding via KEK/DEK).
- **Colorado AI Act (SB 24-205)** – Duty of reasonable care and 90-day AG reporting.
- **Washington My Health My Data Act** – Health data consent and retention.
- **ISO/IEC 42001** – AI management system governance.
- **CEN/CENELEC JTC 21 prEN 18286** – QMS for EU AI Act conformity assessment.

Implementation details and mappings live in:

- `docs/compliance-mapping.md` – Control → clause mappings and evidence pointers.
